About me and this project

My name is Domenic Cassisi, I am a 22 years old computer science student from Germany. During my semester abroad in Edinburgh, Scotland, I visited Napier University and enrolled for Web Technologies. In this module you learn the fundamentals of client-side web development, covering the most important aspects of HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

This project is the result of my coursework. Since I have a passion for developing games, I decided to develop a simple game using tools I never worked with before. The whole project was a really exciting experience. I might even work on it sometime in the future.
